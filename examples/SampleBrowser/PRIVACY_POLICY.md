# Privacy Policy for Sokol.NET Sample Browser

**Effective Date:** February 2, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

## Overview

Sokol.NET Sample Browser ("the App") is a graphics demonstration application that showcases various rendering techniques, physics simulations, and animation capabilities using the Sokol.NET framework. This privacy policy describes how the App handles user information.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

The Sokol.NET Sample Browser:
- Does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII)
- Does not require user registration or login
- Does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or other sensitive device data
- Does not use analytics services or tracking tools
- Does not display advertisements
- Does not transmit any data over the internet

## Data Storage

The App operates entirely offline and locally on your device. Any temporary data generated during runtime (such as rendering buffers or animation states) exists only in device memory and is automatically cleared when you close the App.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) as it does not collect any personal information whatsoever.

**Parents and Guardians:** The App is a safe, educational graphics demonstration tool suitable for children. It contains no advertisements, in-app purchases, or data collection mechanisms.

## Permissions

The App requires the following device permissions:

- **Graphics/Display Access** - Required to render 3D graphics and animations
- **Touch/Input Events** - Required to interact with samples and navigate the menu

These permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality and do not involve any data collection or transmission.

Since the App does not collect or transmit any data, there is no user data at risk. All processing occurs locally on your device.
